Pension Dashboard

Login

This is a new service - your feedback will help us to improve it.

You can

  • See your pensions all together in one place - including workplace pensions, personal pensions, your State Pension and any you may have lost.
  • See how much you could get when you retire.

How it works

  1. Prove your identity using GOV.UK One Login. You can set one up as part of this tool.
  2. Enter you National Insurance number, if you know it. You can still proceed without one.
  3. See all the pensions that match your details, including your estimated income when you retire.
  4. Come back any time to view your Pension Dashboard and latest figures.

You'll need

  • a mobile phone
  • your current address and previous addresses you know
  • photo ID, if it’s your first time using GOV.UK One Login
  • your National Insurance number, if you know it

We’ll share the information from GOV.UK One Login with pension providers to find your pensions. We will not store your data. Learn more in our privacy notice.
